Welcome everyone! Today we are diving into the world of stress management, a crucial skill in our fast-paced lives. Let's undertand the nature of stress and explore one tool to effectively manage it leading to a more balanced and fulfilling life.

A. What is Stress?

Stress is defined as a state of emotional or physical tension. This is also your body's response to a challenge. Stress can be positive but when its chronic, it can be detrimental to your health.
