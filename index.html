Stress Less, Live More - A Stress Management Essentials

Ready to rewrite your stress story? Embark on a transformative journey with "Stress Less, Live More" stress management course. In this short course you will gain practical tools, insights and recognize the importance of self-reflection to help you thrive in the face of stress.

A. Learning Outcome

By the end of this lesson, you will be able to :

  • Reflect the common stressors faced as students.
  • Assess your level of stress using a self-assessment tool.
  • Explain the effects of stress to both mental and physical well-being.
  • Compare three different techniques in managing stress.
  • Apply one stress management technique.
